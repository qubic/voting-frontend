# Clarification on the Qubic listing in GateUS

Qubic is currently listed and available for trading in the United States through Gate.US, a licensed exchange platform operating across several U.S. jurisdictions.

We've been invited to join their NextUp program, a marketing and liquidity initiative designed to boost visibility and trading activity for listed projects.

The program requires providing $100K worth of Qubic, which goes directly to Gate.US users through promotional activities, not to the exchange itself.

Participation in NextUp is described as voluntary. There are no guarantees on permanent listing with or without NextUp campaign but in theory, contribution to the NextUp campaign could decrease our chances of delisting.
